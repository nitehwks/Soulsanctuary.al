# SoulSanctuary - Find Your Sanctuary Within

## Faith-Based Features

SoulSanctuary is a **Christian faith-integrated AI companion** that combines pastoral care with evidence-based therapeutic practices. Our mission is to serve each person in the best way possible - combining the wisdom of faith with proven therapeutic practices.

### Core Faith Features

**Prayer & Spiritual Exercises**
- **Centering Prayer** - Connect with God through sacred words and stillness
- **Scripture Meditation (Lectio Divina)** - Deep, prayerful engagement with God's Word
- **Serenity Prayer Practice** - Finding acceptance, courage, and wisdom
- **Gratitude & Blessing Practice** - Cultivating thanksgiving and blessing others
- **Psalms of Comfort** - Ancient words for modern struggles (Psalm 23, Psalm 91, and more)
- **Faith-Based Affirmations** - Scripture truths to counter anxious thoughts

### Pastoral Care Mode

Our Therapist/Pastoral Care mode combines:
- **Progressive, Modern Church Approach** - Welcoming, non-judgmental, focused on love and grace
- **Professional Therapeutic Techniques** - CBT, DBT, ACT, and Mindfulness
- **Spiritual Guidance** - Prayers, scripture, and faith-based encouragement when appropriate

### Optional Faith Features

We respect that not everyone shares the same faith journey:
- Faith features can be **turned off** for users who prefer secular support only
- When disabled, the AI focuses purely on evidence-based therapeutic techniques
- Smart tracking respects your preferences - after declining spiritual content, we pause offerings

## Evidence-Based Therapy Modules

**DBT (Dialectical Behavior Therapy)**
- TIPP Skills for immediate distress tolerance
- Wise Mind meditation for balanced thinking

**CBT (Cognitive Behavioral Therapy)**
- Thought Records for examining troubling thoughts
- Cognitive Distortion identification and reframing

**Mindfulness**
- Body Scan Meditation for grounding
- Box Breathing for calm

**ACT (Acceptance and Commitment Therapy)**
- Values Clarification exercises

**Grounding Techniques**
- 5-4-3-2-1 Sensory Grounding
