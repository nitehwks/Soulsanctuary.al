A Sanctuary For Your Story

SoulSanctuary is a Christian AI companion that brings together a wise chat bot, mental health tools, and spiritual guidance in one privacy-first experience—so you can be fully honest and fully safe.

SoulSanctuary is designed to be a safe, sacred space for your most vulnerable thoughts, prayers, and struggles. Every conversation is treated like a confidential pastoral visit, not just another data stream.

Privacy with a pastoral heart: Sensitive information is protected with strong AES‑256‑GCM encryption, the same class of protection used to secure highly confidential data. Highly sensitive details such as Social Security and credit card numbers are automatically redacted and never stored at all. Your identity details, including phone number and email, are kept only as encrypted “contact facts,” so who you are is carefully separated from what you share. [file:2]
