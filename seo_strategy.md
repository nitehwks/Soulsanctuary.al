# SEO Strategy

## In scope
- Public marketing page at `/` (unauthenticated landing experience)
- Public pricing / sales page at `/sales`
- Crawl and discovery files (`robots.txt`, `sitemap.xml`, `llms.txt`)
- Shared HTML shell for all public SPA routes

## Out of scope
- Authenticated application routes after sign-in (`/dashboard`, `/settings`, `/docs`, `/addons`, `/groups`, `/analytics`, `/clinician`, `/feature-flags`)
- Admin or internal APIs under `/api/**`
- Native mobile, iOS, Android, and Electron packaging surfaces

## Target audience
- People seeking a Christian faith-integrated AI companion for emotional support and therapeutic tools.

- Christian mental health support
