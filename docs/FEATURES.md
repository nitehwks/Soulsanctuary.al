# SoulSanctuary AI - Features & Capabilities

## Overview

SoulSanctuary is a **Christian faith-integrated AI companion** - your trusted confidant that combines faith-based support with evidence-based therapeutic practices. Available as a web app and native iOS app for iPhone and iPad.

---

## Unique Features

### 1. Dual-Mode System
Switch seamlessly between two specialized modes:

| Mode | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **Chat Mode** | Caring companion for everyday conversations, remembering your story |
| **Therapist Mode** | Supportive care combining faith, therapy techniques, and life coaching |

### 2. Faith Integration (Optional)
- **Prayer Support**: AI offers to pray with you at meaningful moments
- **Scripture Guidance**: Relevant Bible verses for your situation
- **Spiritual Encouragement**: Faith-based affirmations and comfort
- **Denominational Flexibility**: Adapts to your belief system
- **Can Be Disabled**: Full secular support mode available

### 3. Evidence-Based Therapy Modules
Professional therapeutic techniques integrated into conversations:

| Technique | Application |
|-----------|-------------|
| **CBT** (Cognitive Behavioral Therapy) | Thought records, cognitive distortion identification |
| **DBT** (Dialectical Behavior Therapy) | TIPP skills, Wise Mind meditation, distress tolerance |
| **ACT** (Acceptance & Commitment Therapy) | Values clarification exercises |
| **Mindfulness** | Body scan meditation, box breathing |
| **Grounding** | 5-4-3-2-1 sensory technique |

### 4. Crisis Detection & Safety
Real-time protection for vulnerable users:
- **Automatic Detection**: Identifies suicidal ideation, self-harm, abuse, domestic violence
- **Severity Assessment**: None, low, moderate, high, critical levels
- **Crisis Resources**: Immediate connection to hotlines and support
- **Safety Wrapping**: All responses filtered for user safety
- **Compassionate Guidance**: Appropriate spiritual support in crisis moments

### 5. Advanced Privacy & Security
World-class data protection:
- **AES-256-GCM Encryption**: Military-grade encryption for sensitive data
- **PII Redaction**: Automatic removal of SSN, credit cards from storage
- **Tamper-Evident Audit Logs**: Chain-hashed logs for security tracking
- **GDPR Compliant**: Data export and deletion on request
- **Consent Management**: Granular control over data collection
- **Privacy Dashboard**: Visual overview of your data and settings

### 6. Dual AI Model System
Unique multi-model approach for better responses:
- Queries multiple AI models simultaneously
- Selects best response for each conversation
- Real-time model tracking in dashboard
- Automatic fallback for reliability

### 7. Context Memory & Learning
The AI truly knows you:
- **Remembers Details**: Names, preferences, relationships, goals
- **Tracks Patterns**: Recurring themes and concerns
- **Builds Understanding**: Deeper insight over time
- **Knowledge Graph**: Visual map of what the AI knows about you

### 8. Psychological Profiling
Deep understanding of your mental wellness:
- Personality pattern analysis
- Motivation and goal tracking
- Mood observation and assessment
- Personalized coaching plans
